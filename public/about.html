About CivicAlert

A citizen-powered platform connecting communities with the authorities responsible for their welfare.

Our Mission

Bridging Citizens & Government

CivicAlert was built on a simple but powerful belief: when citizens can communicate infrastructure problems clearly and quickly, local governments can fix them faster.

Too often, critical issues — crumbling roads, water outages, failing streetlights, security threats — go unreported or are lost in bureaucratic channels. CivicAlert creates a direct, transparent pipeline from resident to responsible authority, with public accountability built in through our open dashboard.

Every report is timestamped, categorised, and tracked. Every resolution is recorded. The community holds government accountable — not with protests, but with data.

Built to Scale

How CivicAlert Scales to Any District

01
District Onboarding

Any local government area can be added by a system administrator. Each district gets its own issue queue, dashboard view, and authority account.

02
Localised Categories

Issue types are customisable per district. A coastal area may add "Flood Drainage" while a rural district adds "Agricultural Road Access".

03
Multi-language Support

The system is designed to support Yoruba, Hausa, Igbo, and other local languages to ensure inclusive participation across all demographics.

04
Government API Integration

CivicAlert can integrate with existing government systems via REST APIs, enabling automatic ticket creation in internal workflow tools.
