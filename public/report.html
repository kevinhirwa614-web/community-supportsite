Tips for a Good Report

  • Include the exact street name and nearest landmark
  • Describe how long the issue has been present
  • Mention any health or safety risks
  • Add a clear photo taken in daylight if possible
  • Provide your phone number for urgent issues

What Happens Next?

  • Report logged within seconds
  • District authority notified within 1 hour
  • Status updated to "Under Review" within 24h
  • Resolution target: 3–7 working days
  • Track progress on the Dashboard
