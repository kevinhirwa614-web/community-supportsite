Simple Process

How CivicAlert Works

From problem spotted to problem solved — CivicAlert makes community reporting fast, transparent, and effective.

01

Submit a Report

Fill in the issue details — location, type, and description. You can attach a photo as evidence.

02

Issue is Logged

Your report is assigned an ID, timestamped, and forwarded to the relevant district authority within minutes.

03

Track Progress

Monitor your issue on the public dashboard. Status updates from Pending → Under Review → Resolved are posted live.

04

Issue Resolved

When the problem is fixed, the status is updated to Resolved and residents are notified. Community improved.
